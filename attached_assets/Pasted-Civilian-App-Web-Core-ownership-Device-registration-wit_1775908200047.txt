Missing features that would make SPORS exceptional
These don't exist yet but would genuinely set it apart:
Reward system — owner sets a cash reward at time of reporting. Finder gets a Razorpay/UPI payment link automatically when recovery is confirmed. Incentivizes more people to scan actively.
Community trust score — finders build a score over time based on successful recoveries. High-trust finders get surfaced first to owners. Creates a reputation layer.
IMEI blacklist check on registration — before allowing registration, cross-check against CEIR (India's stolen device database). If the IMEI is already blacklisted nationally, reject and alert police automatically.
Bluetooth Low Energy advertising mode — when a user marks their device lost, their phone starts advertising a SPORS beacon even while locked. Currently you rely on finders detecting it — this makes the lost device itself an active participant.
Offline detection queue — finder's app caches detections locally when offline (no network in basement, mall, etc.) and batch-syncs when connectivity returns. Critical for India's patchy networks.
Auto-translate in chat — owner and finder may speak different languages. Groq translates messages in real time. You already have Groq in the stack, this is a small addition with huge real-world impact.
Web companion for owners — full account + device management on the website so users who lost their only phone can still access recovery tools from a browser or borrowed computer.
Police verification badge — verified law enforcement accounts get a badge visible
